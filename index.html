Support & Help Center

Welcome to Brainlot Support

Thank you for using Brainlot! We're here to help you get the most out of your learning experience.

Brainlot is an AI-powered learning platform that transforms your study materials into interactive quizzes. Upload PDFs and images to generate MCQs instantly and learn with engaging TikTok-style swipeable flashcards.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload study materials?

You can upload PDFs and images directly from your device:

  1. Open the Brainlot app
  2. Tap the upload button
  3. Select a PDF or image from your library
  4. Wait for the AI to generate MCQs
  5. Start practicing!

What are the upload limits?

  • Free Plan: Up to 3 sessions per day (12 images total)
  • Pro Plan: Up to 500 sessions per day (2000 images) with cloud model, plus unlimited sessions with local model

How do I practice incorrect questions?

After completing a quiz, you'll see your score summary. Tap "Practice Incorrect Questions" to review and improve on the questions you got wrong.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Go to the Subscription screen in the app and tap "Upgrade". Pro plan includes:

  • Up to 500 sessions per day (2000 images) with cloud model
  • Unlimited sessions with local model
  • Unlimited MCQ generation
  • Ad-free experience
  • Priority support

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • iOS: Go to Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Brainlot ‚Üí Cancel Subscription
  • Android: Go to Google Play Store ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Brainlot ‚Üí Cancel

I'm having trouble signing in

You can sign in using:

  • Email and password
  • Google Sign-In
  • Apple Sign-In

If you're experiencing issues, try signing out and signing back in, or contact support.

How accurate are the generated MCQs?

Our AI uses advanced machine learning to generate high-quality MCQs from your study materials. While we strive for accuracy, we recommend reviewing the questions and correcting any errors if needed.

Technical Support

App Not Working?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Close and restart the app
  2. Update to the latest version from the App Store or Google Play
  3. Check your internet connection
  4. Clear app cache (Android) or reinstall the app
  5. Make sure you're signed in to your account

Upload Issues

If you're having trouble uploading files:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure the file size is not too large (recommended: under 10MB)
  • Try a different file format or image
  • Check your subscription status (Free plan has limits)
